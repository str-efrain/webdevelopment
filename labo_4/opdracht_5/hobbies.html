I have travelled to tons of countries, 26 in total, you can check out a map of my travels here. Two years ago I also picked up a new hobby, photography, and I have since taken my camera on a couple of my trips. I regularly post the pictures I took of these trips on my dedicated Instagram page.

Music

Music is one of my biggest hobbies as it goes along with all the previous ones, I listen to music when exercising, travelling and pretty much anything else. I've been playing the electric and acoustic guitar for only two months now, mainly electric, but have learned lots of songs. As for piano, I have been playing on and off for about three years. I bought all my music equipment and instruments at Thomann, they are the largest seller of anything that has something to do with music.
